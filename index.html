GOAL STATEMENT

This purpose of the application is to create a social community of book lovers around certain genres or a specific book, to help book lovers find books that are highly recommended by other book lovers who have similar interests and to enable book lovers to find nearby or online communities or social groups relating to the book they love.



USER ANALYSIS

The users of this system would be individuals of any age who love reading, have basic level technical skills and are looking to add a social component to their reading hobby.
